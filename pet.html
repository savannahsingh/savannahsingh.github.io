Adopt a Pet



Net Art, 2019

Many animals end up in shelters all over the world. Sometimes they are pets that have ran off and other times, they are stray animals. In my net art project, we will be following 4 different pets. We will see what happens to them after they are captured and placed in one of Peta's shelters.
