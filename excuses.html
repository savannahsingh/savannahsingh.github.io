Excuses





Digital Video, 2018

This video is about global warming and how people act towards it. Humanity often displaces the blame and rarely takes responsibility for their own actions. While majority of global warming is caused by major companies polluting the environment, it doesn't mean we can't do little things in our everyday life to make the world a cleaner place. When it comes time to "pay" it would be easier on everyone to split the responsibility, the same way a group of friends might split a check.
