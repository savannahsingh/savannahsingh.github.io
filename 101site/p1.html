Project 1

For my drawing tool, I made different tools using randomness and RGB. I wanted to make it so that every time you draw something it will always look slightly different due to the unpredicability of the tools. Lines, ellipses, and squares can be made with this tool. The color options are red, green, blue, grayscale, and rainbow colors.


Key:

1: Black Pen | 2: blue circles | 3: green circles | 4: red circles | 5: rainbow circles | 6: grayscale circles | 7: grayscale line | 8: red line | 9: green line | 0: blue line | q: rainbow line | w: grayscale squares | r: red squares | t: green square | y: blue squares | u: rainbow square
