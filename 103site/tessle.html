Group Artist Statement

Originally a neural network design that focused on collective thoughts, our group then decided to focus on a more specific part of the brain: emotions. Each piece and shape represents a certain emotion a person feels throughout the day. In order to create a sculptural representation of one’s mood, we decided to use Design Option 2 which is 3d Style to create a free standing structure. Overall, this is a three-dimensional mood tracker.
