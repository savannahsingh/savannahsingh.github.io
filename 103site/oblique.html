This is an animation I made in blender for Art 102. I did all of the modeling and the animation. The idea for this came from my D&D game with my friends. We were on a pirate ship in the game. The idea for the sword fight came from another scene I made in blender that involved a samurai avocado and a ninja toast that were getting ready to fight.

This is a personal piece. I made this as concept art for a story I have been working on for about 2 years. This was the first time I was drawing a full illustration for the story instead of just sketches.

This is a visual novel game I made in Unity for Art 108, the game is called Last Call. In the game, the player is a bartender who is actually a guardian angel in disguise and their job is to convince people to continue their life before. The idea for the game came from my boyfriend and I talking about death and what happens to people when they are having the moment where their life flashes before their eyes.
