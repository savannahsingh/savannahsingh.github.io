Pikachu Model and Print



3D Print, 2019

Model made in Minecraft.

Pokemon has been a world wide phenomenon for over 2 decades, and it is the mascot is one of the most recognizable video games in the industry. I chose Pikachu because it is one of my favorite of all the hundreds of Pokemon. Pokemon has been apart of many of our lives growing up and has had an influence on us.
